Number of Victims

Studies indicate that the rate at which public mass shootings occur has tripled since 2011. Between 1982 and 2011, a mass shooting occurred roughly once every 200 days. However, between 2011 and 2014, that rate has accelerated greatly with at least one mass shooting occurring every 64 days in the United States.
Each circle represents a shooting, click on the circle to access the news of that case!

Number of Victims

Acoording to a study by Adam Lankford, in the United States, there are 6.87 victims on average per incident. In the other 171 countries the average was 8.8 victims per incident.

Comparing the numbers

This chart can help you to compare the number of occurrences, victims and fatalities of each state. Each circle represents a state, the larger the circle the greater the number of deaths.
